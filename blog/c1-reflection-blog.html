C1: Reflection blog

Based on Fireside Chat with Shereef: Kitchen vs. Table

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

It is completely foreign to me. I never heard the concept of "mindset" before but it completely make sense.

What are your impressions?

Now that heard that there are different mindset, I'll try my best to have a creator mindset. Also when Shareef mentioned that "intelligence" as we normally know it i.e. smartness is less important than "emotional" intelligence such as: Persistence, humility, empathy, how to tolerate my frustation, the ability to reflect, self awareness/mindfullness, curiosity, integrity and learning to solicit feedback.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

As mentioned, we are used to the customer/restaurant mindset so we need to train ourselves for the creator mindset. It's been quite awhile since I've been in the environment of "group learning" so I need to get familiar with how to solicit feedback from my cohorts especially since I am an introvert.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

I don't think so. I expect I would struggle occasionaly in this "new environment" but with the struggle, one can use it as an opportunity to grow careerwise and lifewise (is that a word?).

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

Definitely excited and nervous at the same time. Nervous because it is a huge change from "normal" life but excited for the future!
