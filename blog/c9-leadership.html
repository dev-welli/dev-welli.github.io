C9: Leadership

What is your experience of compassionate (or not) leadership?

I might be lucky that I never had a manager/boss that is not compassionate. By being compassionate, it makes me care more about my performance at the workplace rather than just seing my job as a chore.

Do the multiplying goodness meditation. How did you find it?

At times, I still find it difficult to meditate (properly), my mind still wonder so need more practice. The idea is certainly good but quite hard to execute.

How does the SCARF model apply to your Phase 0 experience so far? How will it apply to bootcamp?

Reading the SCARF chapter, I do notice some that come into play during the phase 0 period. Such as Status when I (hopefully) improve some skills.

Or Certainty, regarding job security when you need to take a long leave to do boot camp. Relatedness with making new EDA friends where we are undergoing the same experience.

Have a difficult conversation. How did it go? (It's a valuable skill, but can take practice to develop!)

I think the hardest part of the "difficult conversation" is the beginning, bringing the subject and start the conversation itself.

Once the conversation started, it flows more easily. There were some tears but it makes us closer.
