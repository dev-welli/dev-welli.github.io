C2: Emotional Intelligence

Based on Chade-Meng Tan's Search Inside Yourself: The Unexpected Path to Achieving Success, Happiness (And World Peace)

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

I did not realise (before joining EDA) that "Emotional Intelligence" is a thing. I mean I know it is important to be kind, considerate person but I did not know it is imperative to have it to be succcessful in work and more imporantly in life.

Emotional intelligence is the best predictors of success at work and fulfillment in life

Probably one thing that I took from the chapter is that emotional intelligence is trainable for everyone, something that can be acquired with practice.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Quite hard. Tried it when reading the chapter 1 but my mind wander and start thinking about other things. I need to keep practicing on it, probably easier at the end of the day before I go to bed.
